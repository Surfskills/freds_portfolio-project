AirBnB School Project

A school project in HTML that replicates the frontend interface of Airbnb, the popular online platform for vacation rentals and accommodations. This educational endeavor allowed me to apply HTML, CSS, and potentially JavaScript skills to create a web-based user interface reminiscent of Airbnb's website.

Trainees Management System

An elegantly designed full-stack web application developed to facilitate the efficient management of interns throughout their tenure within the organization. This comprehensive platform empowers supervisors with the tools and functionalities necessary to streamline the oversight of intern activities, fostering a productive and seamless experience for both interns and the company

Human Design Thinking Project

A design thinking prototype aimed at mitigating ICT skills disparities among the youth in Kibera systems. This innovative solution seeks to bridge the digital divide by fostering accessible and inclusive ICT education, empowering the youth in Kibera with essential skills for the modern digital landscape. Through thoughtful design and user-centric approaches, the prototype strives to create meaningful impact by providing tailored educational experiences, thereby fostering a more technologically literate and empowered generation in the community

IM Portal Revamp

A comprehensive overhaul of an incident management portal aimed to automate root cause analysis (RCA) initiation and enhance the user interface. This project aimed to streamline and expedite the RCA process while elevating the user experience through a redesigned interface. By implementing automation and user-centered design principles, this revamp seeked to improve the efficiency of incident resolution and provide a more intuitive and efficient platform for users.
